This Privacy Notice for BusLK ("we", "us", or "our") explains how we handle information when you use our website and services, including:

Questions or concerns? If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us at shaanilf18@gmail.com .

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
Information you provide to us
We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details to use BusLK. The only personal information we may receive is what you voluntarily send to us (for example, if you email us with a question or feedback). In that case, we will use that information only to respond to your message.
Sensitive information
We do not request or intentionally process any sensitive personal information (such as information about health, religion, or political opinions).
Information collected automatically (server logs)
When you visit our website, our hosting provider (Vercel) automatically collects certain technical information in server logs. This may include:
  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages you visit and the time and date of your visit
  • Error and performance data
This information is used for security, to operate our website, and to understand basic usage (such as how many people visit). We do not use this information to personally identify you.

2. DO WE USE COOKIES OR TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES?
BusLK itself does not set cookies for analytics, login, or tracking.
However, we have integrated Google AdSense code on our website. Even if no ads are currently displayed, Google may use cookies or similar technologies to collect technical information about your device and usage for advertising-related purposes. These technologies are controlled by Google and are subject to Google’s own privacy policy, not ours.
For more information about how Google collects and uses data, please see Google’s Privacy Policy (“Google Privacy & Terms”) on their official website.

3. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?
We use the limited information we have for the following purposes:
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and Services
  • To protect the security and integrity of the website (for example, detecting abuse or attacks)
  • To respond to messages you send us (such as support or feedback emails)
We do not sell your personal information.

4. WHEN DO WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION?
We do not share your personal information with third parties except:
  • With our service providers that host and run the website (for example, Vercel)
  • With Google, through the AdSense code embedded on our website
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process
These third parties are only given access to information they need to perform their services.

5. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?
We only keep information for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Server logs are retained according to our hosting provider’s policies. Emails you send us may be kept for a reasonable period to handle your request and maintain our records.
