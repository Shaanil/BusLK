About Us
BusLK is a modern digital platform dedicated to simplifying public transportation across Sri Lanka. Our goal is to provide users with easy access to bus schedules, routes, timings, and nearby bus stops — all in a clear and convenient format.
We understand that real-time bus information can often be inconsistent or difficult to find. That’s why BusLK is built to help users make more informed travel decisions. While the information shown may not always be 100% accurate due to ongoing route and schedule changes, we work continuously to offer the most reliable and updated details possible.
Our platform is designed for everyone — daily commuters, students, travelers, tourists, and anyone navigating Sri Lanka’s bus network. We believe that when people have access to better information, their journeys become easier, safer, and more enjoyable.
At BusLK, our vision is to become the leading digital guide for public transport information in Sri Lanka, enhancing mobility and helping communities stay connected.
